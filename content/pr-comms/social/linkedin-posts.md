### Post 6: The Accuracy Problem (Solved)
---
"I don't trust AI for PR work. What if it hallucinates?"

Valid concern. Here's the solution:

**Grounded tools.**

Standard ChatGPT draws from its entire training data.
Grounded tools (like NotebookLM) ONLY access sources you provide.

The difference:
- Ungrounded: "Based on my knowledge..." (gambling)
- Grounded: "Based on the documents you uploaded..." (research)

For PR work, this matters enormously:
- Media monitoring → Upload the actual coverage
- Client research → Upload verified company information
- Competitive analysis → Upload confirmed competitor data

Zero hallucination risk when the AI can only reference what you gave it.

This is how we teach AI for PR:
1. Perplexity for finding sources
2. NotebookLM for synthesizing them
3. ChatGPT for writing the output

Grounded research. Reduced risk.

#GroundedAI #PRAccuracy #NoHallucinations

### Post 31: The Grounded Research Stack
---
The PR research stack that eliminates hallucinations:

**Step 1: Perplexity** (Find sources)
- Grounded search with citations
- Identifies authoritative sources
- Provides links for verification

**Step 2: NotebookLM** (Synthesize sources)
- Upload only verified sources
- AI can ONLY reference what you provided
- Zero hallucination risk

**Step 3: ChatGPT** (Write output)
- Draft from synthesized research
- Human refinement for nuance
- Collaborative iteration to polish

The result:
- Cited sources
- Verified information
- Professional output
- No gambling on accuracy

This is how we teach AI for PR.
Not hoping for accuracy. Designing for it.

#GroundedResearch #PRMethodology #NoHallucinations

### Post 32: The POP Framework for PR
---
One framework. Every PR task. Any AI platform.

**P - Persona**
Who should AI be?
"You are a senior PR strategist with 15 years of experience in crisis communications."

**O - Objective**
What specifically needs to happen?
"Analyze this coverage and identify the 5 most important themes for our client briefing."

**P - Parameters**
How should it deliver?
"Present findings in bullet points, lead with the most significant theme, include specific quotes from coverage."

This works in ChatGPT. Claude. Gemini. Custom GPTs.

Why? Because these aren't platform tricks.
They're principles of clear communication.

Better prompts = better outputs.
Every time.

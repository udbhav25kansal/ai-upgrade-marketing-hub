## ASPIRATIONAL STORY 1: Sarah Chen — Solo Practitioner to Strategic Counselor

**Background:**
- Solo family law practitioner, 8 years experience
- Serving primarily middle-income clients
- Drowning in client intake, initial consultations, document prep
- 60+ hour weeks, limited time for strategic casework

**Before The Course:**
- 45 minutes per client intake call
- Manual conflict checks across scattered records
- Generic intake forms requiring extensive follow-up
- Burning out on administrative overhead

**Projected Transformation:**
- **Week 2:** Built client intake assistant with structured question flows
- **Week 3:** Created conflict check system using practice management data
- **Week 4:** Developed case assessment framework based on her expertise
- **Week 5:** Built client portal with secure document upload (no AI processing of client data)
- **Week 6:** Capstone: Complete intake-to-matter system maintaining confidentiality

**Capstone Project:**
Client intake and case assessment system:
- Structured intake questions before consultation
- Automated conflict identification (local processing only)
- Preliminary case type classification
- Matter folder generation with confidentiality protocols
- Time saved: 20+ minutes per client

**Key Insight:**
> "I'm not using AI to replace my judgment. I'm using it to clear space FOR my judgment."

**Marketing Angle:**
Solo practitioners can't hire associates. But they can build AI assistants that handle the work that keeps them from strategic counseling. Sarah reclaimed 10+ hours per week for the legal work only she can do.

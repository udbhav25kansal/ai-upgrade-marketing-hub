## üõ†Ô∏è How to Use This Toolkit

### Step 1: Choose Your Program(s)
Identify which of the 7 programs you're promoting. If you're teaching multiple, focus content by vertical.

### Step 2: Select Your Content
Browse your personalized folder (`03-`, `04-`, or `05-`) and choose:
- 2 blog posts per program
- 5 LinkedIn posts per program
- 3 email templates per program

### Step 3: Customize (Optional)
All content is ready to use as-is, but you can:
- Add personal anecdotes
- Update with recent student examples
- Include cohort-specific details (dates, pricing, etc.)

### Step 4: Grab Graphics
Go to `01-graphics-specs/` and download:
- Course graphics for your program(s)
- YouTube thumbnails (if creating video content)
- Blog headers
- Social media images

### Step 5: Distribute Discount Codes
Follow the strategy in `02-discount-strategy/` to send codes to your network using the email templates provided.

### Step 6: Schedule & Post
Use Buffer, Hootsuite, or schedule directly on LinkedIn. Spread content over 3-4 weeks leading up to launch.

### Step 7: Track Results
Log discount code redemptions in the tracking spreadsheet. Share insights with the team for optimization.

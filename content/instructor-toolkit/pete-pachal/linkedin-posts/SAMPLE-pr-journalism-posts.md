## POST 5: The POP Framework (Because Generic Prompts Fail)

Generic prompts = generic outputs.

Specific prompts = useful outputs.

**The POP Framework:**

**P - Persona:** Who should the AI be?
(Not "PR pro" but "Senior crisis comms specialist with healthcare experience")

**O - Objective:** What exactly do you need?
(Not "write a pitch" but "draft opening paragraph for Sarah Frier at Bloomberg about patient care AI, emphasizing data security")

**P - Parameters:** What are the constraints?
- Tone: Professional but approachable
- Length: 2-3 sentences max
- Must include: HIPAA compliance
- Must avoid: "Revolutionary," "game-changing"
- Format: Email-friendly

This level of specificity transforms AI outputs from generic to client-ready.

Master this once. Use it across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, any text AI.

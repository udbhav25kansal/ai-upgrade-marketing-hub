## üé§ Brand Voice Guidelines

### The Upgrade AI Brand Voice
- **Systematic, not hype-driven:** Evidence-based claims, realistic timelines
- **Build-focused, not theory-focused:** Students ship working tools, not just learn concepts
- **Platform-agnostic:** Teach frameworks that transfer, not specific tool buttons
- **Capstone-driven:** Real outcomes, not certificates
- **Honest about AI limits:** Teach weaknesses (hallucinations, bias) as rigorously as strengths

### 4. Maya Bruck — From Producer to Critical AI Thinker

**Background:**
- Film/media producer with UX background
- Experience in user-centered design
- Looking to integrate AI into production workflow

**Before The Course:**
- Skilled at traditional production workflows
- Uncertain how AI fits into creative process
- Concerned about quality and validation

**The Transformation:**
- **Week 3:** Delivered session's most important insight about AI outputs
- **Week 5:** Developed ChatGPT prompt engineering strategy (pre-refine prompts before using paid tools)
- **Week 5:** Partnered with Dimitri to build his dashboard using her credits
- **Week 6:** Capstone: Video script generator assistant with brand voice integration

**Critical Contribution:**
Maya's observation became core course teaching:
> "It can *look* really good. You can say, 'Wow, that looks great.' But in reality, we don't yet know who the target viewer is, what they care about, why they need to be here."

**Key Strategy Shared:**
The "ChatGPT first" approach—use free ChatGPT to refine prompts before spending credits on Lovable.dev. Saved classmates money while improving outputs.

**Marketing Angle:**
UX thinking + AI = powerful combination. Maya didn't just learn AI tools—she taught her cohort how to use them more strategically. Critical evaluation is the skill that makes AI useful.

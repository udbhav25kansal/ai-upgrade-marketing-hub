## POST FORMATTING NOTES

### Hook Patterns That Work
- Direct statement (no preamble)
- Surprising statistic
- Industry reality check
- Future scenario

### Length Guidelines
- Sweet spot: 150-200 words
- Maximum: 250 words
- Under 100 feels thin
- Over 300 loses attention

### Hashtag Strategy
- 2-3 maximum or none
- Industry-specific (#JournalismTwitter, #MediaIndustry)
- Avoid generic (#AI, #News)

### Image Pairing
- Text posts work fine alone
- Screenshots of tools add credibility
- Before/after workflow diagrams perform well
